Autonomous vehicle mapless navigation system

Navigation system for an autonomous vehicle, making it possible not using a high-resolution map, and seeking to be as autonomous as possible. This project is developed with Python and Cython to increase performances in real time. Our approach includes three main modules:

  • Detection of vertical signs (signs, traffic lights) and decision making based on this information

  • Calculation of the trajectory to follow from the markings on the ground

  • Vehicle control from a trajectory in space
